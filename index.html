SPACE: Subspace Projected Attribute Control & Editing

Zero-Shot Voice Conversion via Geometric Disentanglement

Anonymous Author(s)

Under Review for ACL/ICASSP 202X

Abstract

We present SPACE, a novel framework for zero-shot voice attribute editing. Unlike previous methods that rely on black-box generation, SPACE explicitly disentangles gender and accent subspaces within the WavLM feature space. Below are the demonstrations for Accent Conversion and Gender Conversion tasks.

Task 1: Accent Conversion

In this task, we convert the source speaker's accent to the target accent (Indian / South African) while preserving the source speaker's timbre and identity.
