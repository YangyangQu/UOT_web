SPACE: Subspace Projected Attribute Control & Editing

Zero-Shot Voice Conversion via Geometric Disentanglement

Anonymous Author(s)1

1Under Review for ACL/ICASSP 202X

Abstract

We present SPACE, a novel framework for zero-shot voice attribute editing. Unlike previous methods that rely on black-box generation, SPACE explicitly disentangles gender and accent subspaces within the WavLM feature space. By leveraging Unbalanced Optimal Transport (UOT) for geometric steering, our method achieves superior disentanglement and content preservation compared to KNN-VC and VITS-based approaches.
